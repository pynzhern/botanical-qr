Papilionanthe Miss Joaquim — anatomical study

Singapore's national flower (the P. hookeriana × teres hybrid). A schematic study of the real three-dimensional form — flower face, profile (facing direction + spur), and whole-plant habit — drawn to the measured botanical description, to ground the voxel-QR rebuild.

2 · Flower, profile — facing direction & spur

In the QR toy the face is laid flat, pointing at the sky so it projects to a scannable code — the one deliberate departure from the outward-facing reality (keeps the projection = scan rule intact).

3 · Whole plant — climbing habit & raceme

Flowers are clustered on one apical spike facing outward — not scattered over the whole plant. Blooms only after the stem climbs ~40–50cm above its support (≈1.4–1.5m tall).
